Privacy Statement

Vexra is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Statement outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data Controller

Vexra acts as the data controller for the personal data we collect and process. This means we determine the purposes and means of processing your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal data:

  • Contact information (name and email address)
  • Financial information (e.g., investment preferences, portfolio details)
  • Compliance-related information (e.g., responses to MiFID and ESG questionnaires)

3. Purpose of Data Processing

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage our services
  • To ensure compliance with regulatory requirements (e.g., MiFID and ESG)
  • To communicate with you and respond to your inquiries
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To conduct data analysis and research

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Your consent
  • Performance of a contract
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate interests pursued by Vexra

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal data with:

  • Service providers and partners who assist us in delivering our services
  • Regulatory authorities, if required by law
  • Other third parties, with your consent or as necessary to provide our services

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Our security practices are regularly reviewed and updated to ensure the highest level of protection.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Statement, comply with legal obligations, and resolve any disputes.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can request details of personal information that we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal limitations.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request the restriction of processing your personal data.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request the transfer of your data to another organization.
